What is PCM Stream?

PCM stands for Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. PCM stream is a popular choice for students who want to pursue a career in engineering, architecture, or other science-related fields. This stream is highly regarded as it provides a strong foundation in science and mathematics, which are essential for many careers.

Subjects in PCM Stream

The main subjects in PCM stream are:

  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Mathematics

In addition to these subjects, students may also study other subjects such as English, Computer Science, and Physical Education.

Career Options in PCM Stream

Students who choose PCM stream have a wide range of career options available to them, including:

  • Engineering
  • Architecture
  • Astronomy
  • Computer Science
  • Finance
  • Research and Development

Many universities and colleges offer undergraduate and graduate programs in these fields, and there are many job opportunities available for graduates with a strong background in science and mathematics.
