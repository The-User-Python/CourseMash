Principles for Solving PCM Questions

Here are some principles to keep in mind when solving PCM questions:

  • Always read the question carefully and make sure you understand what is being asked.
  • Identify the given information and make a list of what is known and unknown.
  • Use appropriate formulas and equations to solve the problem.
  • Pay attention to units and make sure your answer is in the correct unit.
  • Check your answer and make sure it makes sense in the context of the problem.

By following these principles, you can improve your problem-solving skills and achieve better results in PCM.
