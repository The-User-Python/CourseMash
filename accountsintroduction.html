Commerce Stream

The Commerce stream is a popular choice for students who are interested in business, finance, and economics. This stream prepares students for a variety of careers in the corporate world, including accounting, marketing, human resources, and more.

Subjects Covered in the Commerce Stream

  • Accountancy
  • Economics
  • Business Studies
  • Mathematics
  • Informatics Practices (optional)
  • Entrepreneurship (optional)

Career Options

Students who pursue the Commerce stream can look forward to a wide range of career opportunities, such as:

  • Accountant
  • Financial Analyst
  • Marketing Manager
  • Human Resources Manager
  • Business Consultant
  • Entrepreneur
