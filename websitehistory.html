Website History

CourseMash.net launched in 2023 with the aim to provide high-quality totally free of cost online courses to students in india as well as across the world. The website was founded by a team of experienced software developer's who wanted to make education accessible and affordable in a free of cost manner for everyone , everywhere around the world.

Since its inception, CourseMash has grown to become one of the most popular online learning platforms, with thousands of students enrolled in various courses. The website offers courses in a wide range of subjects, including PCM, PCB, and Commerce.

CourseMash is committed to continuing its mission of providing high-quality education to students across the world, and is constantly expanding its course offerings and improving its platform to make learning even more accessible and engaging.
